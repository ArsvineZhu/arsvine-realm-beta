I'm glad to meet you here.

Even though we may never have met, I still hope that through these words, works, and scattered records, you can come to know a little about the life of a stranger: what he once cared about, what he tried, and how, amid passion and hesitation, he slowly built a way of expressing himself.

After a period of development, organization, and repeated revision, my personal portfolio website has finally entered its trial run. It is, of course, still far from mature, and many parts of it remain rough around the edges. But to me, this place is already more than a page for displaying my work. It is a carefully arranged corner of the internet: a place to store my projects, interests, thoughts, aesthetics, and those unfinished attempts that are still worth recording.

I will do my best to maintain it, update it, and look after it. And I hope that when you happen to arrive here, it may feel like pushing open an inconspicuous door, and briefly seeing someone who is trying to live, learn, create, and little by little write his own world into being.
